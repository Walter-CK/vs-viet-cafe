About us

A neighbourhood bakery with Vietnamese heart.

Logan Reserve Bakehouse is built around the kind of food people come back for: warm bread, flaky pastries, honest pies, crisp salad rolls and banh mi made with fresh herbs and generous fillings.

We are proud to be part of the local rhythm, from early school runs and tradie coffees to weekend family lunches. Step in, say hello and leave with something fresh from the oven.
